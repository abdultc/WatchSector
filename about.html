Company Overview

WatchSector, powered by Thoratech, is a global leader in 24/7 virtual CCTV monitoring and surveillance solutions. We specialize in providing round-the-clock security monitoring services that protect your properties, people, and assets across the globe. Unlike traditional security companies, we don't sell cameras — we maximize the potential of your existing surveillance infrastructure.

Our unique approach combines trained human operators with advanced AI systems to deliver real-time threat detection and response. Whether you're managing a residential complex in India, a retail chain in the USA, or industrial facilities in Australia, our team ensures comprehensive security coverage through intelligent monitoring and proactive threat prevention.
